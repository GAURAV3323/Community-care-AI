Health that finds you, before the crisis does.

CommunityCare AI helps elderly, low-income, and underserved residents get first-line health guidance, stay on top of medication, and check in on their wellbeing — while giving local health workers a live pulse on their community.

01 · Symptom & Wellness Chat

Describe how you feel. Get clear next steps.

A plain-language triage assistant — not a diagnosis — that tells residents whether to seek care now, book a visit soon, or manage safely at home.

Chest pain + breathless Mild headache, tired Feeling low lately

This tool gives general guidance only and does not replace professional medical advice. In a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.

2
